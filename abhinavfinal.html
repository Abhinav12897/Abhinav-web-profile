  • Blood Bank Management System

    The purpose of the blood bank management system is to simplify and automate the process of searching for blood in case of emergency and maintain the records of blood donors, acceptors, blood donation programs and blood stocks in the bank. The goals and objectives of the Blood Bank Management System are as follows:
    1. To provide a means for the blood bank to publicize and advertise blood donation programs.
    2. To allow the probable recipients to make search and match the volunteer donors, and make request for the blood.
    3. To provide an efficient donor and blood stock management functions to the blood bank by recording the donor and blood details.
    4. To provide synchronized and centralized donor and blood stock database.
    5. To provide immediate storage and retrieval of data and information.
    Blood bank management system will have various relations to store data about various blood banks, donors, acceptors of blood. The first category of management system contains details for donor. The interested donor will be asked to give information about blood group, age, etc. The next category of system has blood bank details with location, quantity, blood groups, etc . Whereas the last category of management system has the blood acceptors details. The acceptor has to give complete details about his age, blood group and the payment details of the acceptor.
